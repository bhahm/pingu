Easy Sharing. Easy Privacy.

Hapsy lets you tell friends where you are, while controlling how much you share. When you post your location, only your friends can see it. On top of that, after 30 minutes your post will fade entirely from the map (and our servers).

Who We Are

We are a team of designers and programmers working to make something useful. Hapsy is the result of months of brainstorming, and years of frustrating searches for our friends. Be it at concerts, the airport, or even the bar, finding our friends always seems to be more difficult than it should be.
